A crypto donation platform for streamers, built on the TON blockchain. Runs as a Telegram Mini App. Viewers connect their wallet and donate directly. Streamers get instant withdrawals, no middleman fees, no platform lock-in. Built from zero to working prototype in 48 hours during a hackathon. Team of four: three developers and one UX/UI designer.

## Challenges

48 hours to go from idea to a working demo on stage. That time pressure forces you to be ruthless about scope. We kept it to the core flow: wallet connect, donate, withdraw. Everything else was roadmap.

Existing donation platforms like Donatello and StreamLabs charge high fees, are disconnected across streaming platforms, and don't touch crypto. Building a TON-native alternative that actually feels simple for both viewers and streamers was the main design challenge.

## Tech stack

- TON blockchain for payments and wallet integration
- Next.js and Telegram Mini App for the frontend
- Python for the backend
- Figma for UX/UI design
